Privacy Policy for Rime

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Rime ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile application.

Introduction

Rime ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Phone number (required for account creation)
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) verification codes sent via SMS
  • Email address (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • First name (required)
  • Last name (required)
  • Profile picture/avatar (optional)
SMS and Phone Number Usage
  • Your phone number is used solely for account authentication and security
  • We send one-time verification codes via SMS during signup and login
  • SMS messages are sent through a secure toll-free number
  • We do not use your phone number for marketing or promotional purposes
  • We do not share your phone number with third parties
  • By providing your phone number, you consent to receive authentication SMS messages
  • Standard message and data rates may apply from your carrier
  • Verification codes expire after 10 minutes

2. Relationship Information

  • Relationship codes (generated for inviting partners)
  • Names of relationship partners (as provided by you)
  • Relationship membership data
  • Relationship streaks and activity tracking

3. Date Match Information

  • Date ideas you view and vote on (like/dislike)
  • Matched date ideas between you and your partners
  • Your ratings of completed dates (1-5 stars)
  • Optional notes about completed dates
  • Date completion status and timestamps

4. Location Data

  • We access location services to help you discover date venues using Google Places API
  • Location data is used only when you actively search for venues
  • We do not store or track your precise location continuously
  • Location access can be disabled in your device settings

5. Usage Information

  • App interaction data
  • Features you use
  • Session information
  • Error logs and crash reports

6. Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. Provide Core Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity through SMS verification codes
  • Secure your account with two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Enable relationship connections with partners
  • Generate and display date suggestions
  • Track date completions and ratings
  • Facilitate real-time synchronization between partners

2. Improve User Experience

  • Personalize date recommendations
  • Show relevant venue suggestions based on your location
  • Maintain relationship streaks and achievements
  • Provide analytics on your dating activity

3. Communication

  • Send SMS verification codes for account authentication (2FA)
  • Send account-related notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Notify you of app updates

Note: We will never send promotional or marketing SMS messages to your phone number.

4. Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Verify phone number ownership during account creation
  • Protect against unauthorized access through SMS 2FA
  • Prevent fraudulent account creation
  • Detect and prevent abuse
  • Maintain data integrity

Data Storage and Security

Storage Infrastructure

  • All data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL)
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data is encrypted at rest on secure servers
  • Profile images are stored in secure cloud storage with signed URLs

Security Measures

  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) via SMS
  • Phone number verification for account creation
  • Password hashing and encryption
  • Row-level security policies to ensure users only access their own data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • SMS verification codes expire after 10 minutes

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Phone numbers are stored securely and only used for authentication
  • SMS verification codes are not stored after validation
  • You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your personal data including phone number
  • Relationship data is retained to maintain integrity for other relationship members
  • Match and rating data is retained for relationship history

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. Supabase (Database and Authentication)

  • Stores all user, relationship, and match data
  • Handles phone number authentication and SMS delivery
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

2. SMS Service Provider (via Supabase/Twilio)

  • Delivers verification codes to your phone number
  • Used only for account authentication (2FA)
  • Does not store message content after delivery
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

3. Google Places API (Location Services)

  • Used to find and suggest date venues
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Expo (App Development Platform)

  • Facilitates app distribution and updates
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

Data Shared with Relationship Partners

When you join or create a relationship:

  • Your name and profile picture are visible to your relationship partners
  • Your date preferences (likes/dislikes) are visible after you vote
  • Your ratings and notes on completed dates are visible to relationship partners
  • Relationship streak data is shared among all relationship members

We DO NOT

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your phone number with advertisers or third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Send promotional or marketing SMS messages
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without consent
  • Track your location without your permission

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

1. Access Your Data

  • View all personal information we have collected
  • Request a copy of your data

2. Update Your Data

  • Edit your profile information at any time
  • Update your name, email, phone number, or profile picture

3. Delete Your Data

  • Delete your account and associated personal data (including phone number)
  • Note: Shared relationship data may be retained for other members

4. Control Permissions

  • Manage location access in your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications (not SMS authentication codes)
  • Control data sharing with relationship partners

Note: SMS verification codes are required for account security and cannot be disabled.

5. Data Portability

  • Request your data in a machine-readable format

Children's Privacy

Rime is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Real-Time Data Synchronization

The app uses real-time synchronization to:

  • Instantly update match status when partners complete dates
  • Show ratings from all relationship members in real-time
  • Update relationship streaks immediately
  • Reflect profile changes across all connected devices

This requires maintaining persistent connections to our servers while the app is in use.
